These are all from the last week or two. Science jobs are being displaced — but not the hands-on ones. Peter Thiel says the math people are more at risk than the word people. DJI patched it in two days. This is the world we're teaching our students to navigate.

Three jaw-dropping facts to mention:

1. ChatGPT alone has 700 million weekly users — that's 10% of the world's adult population using a single AI tool every week. More than the entire population of Europe.

2. By 2026, 90% of online content may be AI-generated (Europol projection). The internet our students grew up reading was written by humans. The one they'll navigate professionally may not be.

3. AI now beats human experts at reading blood cells for leukemia, and can interpret brain MRIs in seconds. NASA's Perseverance rover drives itself on Mars using AI-planned routes. This isn't just about chatbots — it's transforming medicine and space exploration.

If it all gets to be too much, remember that St. Onuphrius went to live in the forest in the nude. There's always an option.

# How a ProCard Powers AI Research

<div class="grid grid-cols-2 gap-8 mt-8">

<div>

<div class="text-left">

**Old model:**
- Hardware: **$50K–$500K**
- Months to procure
- Obsolete in 2–3 years

</div>

</div>

<div>

<div class="text-left">

**Microservices model:**
- Pay-as-you-go API calls
- **~$100–200/month** on a ProCard
- Scale up or down instantly

</div>

</div>

</div>

<div class="text-2xl italic text-left mt-8" style="color: #2E7D32; border-left: 4px solid #2E7D32; padding-left: 16px;">
The democratization of AI — and it changes what a teaching university can do.
</div>

<!--
Here's something the Foundation should know: I don't need a supercomputer to do cutting-edge AI research. The old model was to spend hundreds of thousands of dollars on hardware that takes a year to procure and is obsolete in three. Today, I use a university ProCard to make micro-payments — a few dollars here and there — to cloud AI services. API calls to Claude, GPT, cloud GPU instances when I need them. My monthly AI research budget is a few hundred dollars. That's the democratization of AI, and it fundamentally changes what a teaching university like CI can accomplish. We can punch well above our weight.
